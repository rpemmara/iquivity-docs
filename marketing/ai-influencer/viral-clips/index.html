    AI Influencer - AI Viral Clips¶

    Automatically extract engaging short clips from long-form video content for social media.

    Overview¶

    AI Viral Clips uses artificial intelligence to analyze long videos and automatically identify the most engaging moments. Transform podcasts, webinars, interviews, and other long-form content into bite-sized viral clips perfect for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and other short-form platforms.

    AI-Powered Clip Detection

    Advanced AI analyzes your video content to identify viral-worthy moments, emotional peaks, key quotes, and engaging segments automatically.

    Best Practices¶

    Maximize Clip Success¶

    Content Selection: - Choose engaging source videos - High-quality audio essential - Clear speech and pronunciation - Valuable, shareable content - Natural energy and enthusiasm

    Technical Quality: - 1080p minimum resolution - Clean, clear audio - Good lighting throughout - Stable camera/minimal shake - Balanced audio levels

    Optimization Tips: - Always include subtitles (80% watch on mute) - Hook in first 3 seconds critical - 30-60 seconds optimal length - Test multiple aspect ratios - Post consistently across platforms

    Platform Strategy: - TikTok: Trending audio, fast-paced - Instagram: Aesthetic, polished - YouTube: Value-driven, searchable - LinkedIn: Professional, insights - Facebook: Relatable, shareable

    Troubleshooting¶

    Common Issues¶

    Video Upload Failed: - Cause: File too large, unsupported format, connection issue - Solution: - Compress video to under 2GB - Convert to MP4 format - Check internet connection - Use URL upload instead - Try splitting into smaller segments

    Poor Clip Quality: - Cause: Low-resolution source, compression artifacts - Solution: - Use higher quality source video - Upload original files (not compressed) - Select higher quality export settings - Avoid multiple re-encodes

    No Clips Generated: - Cause: Video too short, poor audio, no speech detected - Solution: - Ensure video is at least 5 minutes - Check audio track exists and is audible - Verify language selection is correct - Test with different video - Contact support with video details

    Subtitles Inaccurate: - Cause: Poor audio quality, accents, background noise, wrong language - Solution: - Verify correct language selected - Use video with clearer audio - Manually edit subtitles after generation - Remove background music/noise from source - Consider professional audio cleanup

    Processing Taking Too Long: - Cause: Long video, high clip count, server load - Solution: - Reduce number of clips requested - Process during off-peak hours - Split video into segments - Upgrade to priority processing - Check account tier limits

    Tips for Success¶

    Create Viral-Worthy Clips¶

    Source Video Tips: - Quality over quantity in source content - Natural conversation flows best - Authentic emotion resonates - Clear value proposition - Tell complete micro-stories

    Optimization: - Lead with the hook - Fast-paced editing - Clear, readable subtitles - Consistent branding - Add trending music (if applicable)
