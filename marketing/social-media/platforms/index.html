    AI Social Media - Platforms¶

    Connect and manage your social media accounts across multiple platforms for seamless content publishing.

    Overview¶

    The Platforms section allows you to connect and manage multiple social media accounts from a centralized dashboard. Connect Facebook, Instagram, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, and TikTok accounts to publish content across all your channels.

    Multi-Account Support

    You can connect and manage multiple accounts from each platform simultaneously.

    Adding New Accounts¶

    Connection Process¶

    Step 1: Select Platform

    1. Navigate to Platforms page
    2. Choose platform to connect:
    3. Facebook
    4. Instagram
    5. X (Twitter)
    6. LinkedIn
    7. TikTok

    Step 2: Authenticate

    Click "Add New Account" button for your chosen platform

    Step 3: Grant Permissions

    Authorize iQuivity AI to: - Publish posts on your behalf - Read basic profile information - Access page/account analytics - Schedule content

    Step 4: Verify Connection

    • Account appears in list with "Active" status
    • Platform icon displayed
    • Account name shown
    • Creation timestamp recorded

    Platform-Specific Setup¶

    Facebook Connection¶

    Requirements: - Facebook Page (for businesses) - Admin access to the page - Facebook Business account

    Permissions Needed: - pages_manage_posts - Publish to page - pages_read_engagement - Read insights - pages_show_list - List available pages

    Setup Steps: 1. Click "Add New Account" under Facebook 2. Log into Facebook 3. Select page(s) to connect 4. Grant requested permissions 5. Confirm connection

    Instagram Connection¶

    Requirements: - Instagram Business or Creator account - Connected to Facebook Page - Admin access

    Permissions Needed: - instagram_basic - Basic profile access - instagram_content_publish - Publish posts - instagram_manage_insights - View analytics

    Setup Steps: 1. Click "Add New Account" under Instagram 2. Connect via Facebook (Instagram requires Facebook) 3. Select Instagram account 4. Grant permissions 5. Verify connection

    X (Twitter) Connection¶

    Requirements: - X/Twitter account - Verified email address - API access (handled by platform)

    Permissions Needed: - Read and write access - Direct message access (optional)

    Setup Steps: 1. Click "Add New Account" under X 2. Authorize iQuivity AI app 3. Grant permissions 4. Confirm account connection

    LinkedIn Connection¶

    Requirements: - LinkedIn account or Company Page - Admin rights (for company pages)

    Permissions Needed: - w_member_social - Share content - r_basicprofile - Read profile data - r_organization_social - Company page access (if applicable)

    Setup Steps: 1. Click "Add New Account" under LinkedIn 2. Sign in to LinkedIn 3. Grant permissions 4. Select profile or page 5. Confirm connection

    TikTok Connection¶

    Requirements: - TikTok Business account - Verified account - Content creator or business profile

    Permissions Needed: - Video upload permissions - Profile information access - Analytics access

    Setup Steps: 1. Click "Add New Account" under TikTok 2. Log into TikTok 3. Authorize application 4. Grant necessary permissions 5. Verify connection

    Account Limits¶

    Platform Limitations¶

    Different plans may have account limits:

    Free Plan: - Connect up to 3 accounts total - 1 account per platform

    Starter Plan: - Connect up to 10 accounts - Multiple accounts per platform

    Professional Plan: - Connect up to 25 accounts - Unlimited per platform

    Enterprise Plan: - Unlimited accounts - White-label options - Custom integrations

    Troubleshooting¶

    Common Issues¶

    "Unable to Connect Account" - Cause: Platform authentication failed - Solution: Ensure correct login credentials, check platform status, verify permissions

    "Account Disconnected Automatically" - Cause: Token expired or permissions changed - Solution: Click refresh to re-authenticate

    "Posts Not Publishing to Platform" - Cause: Account inactive or permission issue - Solution: Check account status, refresh connection, verify publishing permissions

    "Multiple Accounts Not Showing" - Cause: Platform limitations or configuration issue - Solution: Ensure each account authenticated separately, check plan limits
