    AI Social Media Suite - Dashboard¶

    Centralized hub for managing, automating, and analyzing your social media presence across multiple platforms.

    Overview¶

    The AI Social Media Suite dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your social media activities, connected accounts, and publishing analytics. Personalize and automate your social media posts with AI-powered tools.

    Quick Access

    From the dashboard, you can quickly connect accounts, generate new posts, and monitor your published content across all platforms.

    Dashboard Features¶

    Main Actions¶

    Connect Accounts - Quickly link your social media profiles - Manage multiple accounts across platforms - View account status at a glance

    Generate New Post - Create AI-powered social media content - Multi-platform posting capability - Schedule posts for optimal timing

    Visit BrandCenter - Access brand assets and guidelines - Maintain consistent brand voice - Store templates and media

    Common Dashboard Actions¶

    Managing Connected Accounts¶

    Add New Account: 1. Click "Connect Accounts" 2. Select platform (Facebook, Instagram, X, LinkedIn) 3. Click "Add +" 4. Authenticate with platform credentials 5. Grant necessary permissions

    View Account Status: - Active accounts display with green indicator - Inactive accounts show reconnection prompt - Account details include name and platform icon

    Creating Content¶

    Generate New Post: 1. Click "+ Generate New Post" button 2. Follow the 6-step creation wizard: - Step 1: Platform - Select target social networks - Step 2: Company - Define brand information - Step 3: Campaign - Choose or create campaign - Step 4: Content - Generate AI content - Step 5: Review - Preview and edit - Step 6: Publish - Schedule or publish immediately

    Viewing Analytics¶

    Published Posts Analytics: - Displays line chart with monthly data - Interactive controls for data exploration - Platform-specific filtering - Export capabilities for reporting

    Chart Insights: - Peak Publishing Days - Identify optimal posting times - Engagement Patterns - Understand audience behavior - Content Performance - Compare different post types - Platform Effectiveness - Determine best-performing channels

    Troubleshooting¶

    Common Issues¶

    Dashboard Not Loading: - Check internet connection - Clear browser cache - Try different browser - Contact support if issue persists

    Chart Data Not Displaying: - Ensure accounts are connected - Verify posts have been published - Check date range selection - Refresh page

    Account Connection Failed: - Re-authenticate with platform - Check platform API status - Verify permissions granted - Review account credentials
