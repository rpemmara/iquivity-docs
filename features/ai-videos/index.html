    AI Video Generation¶

    Transform content into engaging videos with AI-powered video generation tools. Create professional videos from text, images, or existing videos with ease.

    AI Video Tools Overview¶

    iQuivity provides multiple AI-powered video generation tools for different needs:

    Video Creation Tools:

    • AI Video To Video - Transform and enhance existing videos with AI
    • AI Video Pro - Advanced text-to-video with multiple AI models
    • AI Persona - Create studio-quality videos with AI avatars and voiceovers
    • AI Video - Text-to-video and URL-to-video generation
    • AI Avatar - AI-powered avatar creation and customization

    Choose the tool that best fits your video production needs.

    AI Video To Video¶

    Transform existing videos with AI upscaling, enhancement, and style transfer.

    Overview¶

    AI Video To Video takes your existing video files and enhances them using AI technology. Perfect for improving quality, changing styles, or transforming video aesthetics.

    Features¶

    Video Enhancement: - Video upscaling (increase resolution) - Quality improvement - Frame interpolation (smoother motion) - Noise reduction - Color correction

    Video Transformation: - Style transfer (apply artistic styles) - Format conversion - Aspect ratio adjustment - Speed modification - Filter application

    Available Upscalers: Based on the screenshot, the following upscaling options are available: - Video Upscaler - General video quality enhancement - Cogvideox 5B - AI-powered video enhancement - Animatediff V2V - Animation-style video transformation - Fast Animatediff Turbo - Quick animation processing

    How to Use AI Video To Video¶

    Step 1: Upload Source Video 1. Navigate to Dashboard → AI VIDEO → AI Video To Video 2. Upload your video file 3. Supported formats: .mp4, .mov, .webm, .gif 4. Review video preview

    Step 2: Select Enhancement Type

    Choose your desired transformation:

    Upscaling Options: - Standard upscale (2x, 4x resolution) - AI-enhanced upscaling - Frame rate increase - Quality restoration

    Style Transfer: - Apply artistic filters - Animation conversion - Aesthetic transformations - Custom style presets

    Step 3: Configure Settings 1. Select upscaler/enhancement method 2. Choose output quality 3. Set processing options 4. Preview settings

    Step 4: Generate Enhanced Video 1. Click "Generate" button 2. Processing time varies by length and settings 3. Preview enhanced result 4. Download or save to gallery

    Use Cases¶

    Video Quality Enhancement: - Upscale old footage to HD/4K - Improve low-quality videos - Restore vintage videos - Enhance smartphone recordings

    Style Transformation: - Convert live-action to animation style - Apply artistic filters - Create unique visual effects - Match brand aesthetics

    Format Optimization: - Convert aspect ratios - Optimize for different platforms - Improve compression - Standardize video quality

    AI Video Pro¶

    Advanced text-to-video generation with multiple cutting-edge AI models.

    Overview¶

    AI Video Pro provides access to the latest AI video generation models, allowing you to create amazing videos from text descriptions using state-of-the-art technology.

    Features¶

    Multiple AI Models: The tool offers several AI video generation models:

    Based on the screenshot, available models include: - Generate video with Sora - Latest OpenAI video model - Generate video with Google VEO - Google's advanced video AI - Generate video with Luma Dream Machine - High-quality cinematic videos - Generate video with Kling - Professional video generation - Generate video with Minimax - Efficient video creation

    Video Generation Options: - Text-to-video creation - Reference image support (optional) - Duration control - Size/resolution selection - Advanced model parameters

    How to Use AI Video Pro¶

    Step 1: Access AI Video Pro 1. Navigate to Dashboard → AI VIDEO → AI Video Pro 2. Enter the AI Video Pro interface

    Step 2: Choose AI Model 1. Click "Select AI Model" dropdown 2. Browse available models: - Sora - Cutting-edge video generation - Google VEO - Advanced AI video - Luma Dream Machine - Cinematic quality - Kling - Professional results - Minimax - Fast generation 3. Select your preferred model

    Step 3: Describe Your Video 1. Enter text description in the prompt field 2. Describe the video you want to generate 3. Be specific about: - Scene and setting - Actions and movements - Visual style - Camera angles - Lighting and mood

    Example Prompts: 

    Good Prompt:
"A cinematic shot of a lighthouse on a rocky coast at sunset,
waves crashing against the rocks, seagulls flying overhead,
warm golden light, camera slowly panning around the lighthouse,
dramatic clouds in the sky"

Basic Prompt:
"A lighthouse by the ocean"

    Step 4: Add Reference Image (Optional) 1. Upload a reference image 2. Image must match requested width and height 3. Supported formats: JPG, PNG 4. AI will use image as visual guide

    Step 5: Configure Settings

    Duration: Select video length from dropdown: - 4 seconds - 8 seconds - 12 seconds - Custom duration (model-dependent)

    Size (Resolution): Choose output resolution: - 720×1280 (Portrait) - 1280×720 (Landscape) - 1080×1920 (Vertical) - Custom dimensions

    Step 6: Generate Video 1. Click "Generate" button 2. Wait for AI processing 3. Generation time: 1-5 minutes depending on model 4. Preview generated video 5. Download or regenerate if needed

    Model Comparison¶

    When to Use Each Model:

    Sora (OpenAI): - Cutting-edge technology - Highly realistic results - Complex scene generation - Best for: Professional content, realistic scenes

    Google VEO: - Advanced AI capabilities - Great for diverse content - Strong visual quality - Best for: Varied content types, experimentation

    Luma Dream Machine: - Cinematic quality - Smooth motion - Artistic results - Best for: Creative projects, film-style videos

    Kling: - Professional output - Reliable results - Good balance of speed and quality - Best for: Business content, consistent quality

    Minimax: - Fast generation - Efficient processing - Good for iterations - Best for: Quick tests, multiple variations

    Use Cases¶

    Marketing & Advertising: - Product demonstrations - Brand stories - Social media ads - Promotional videos

    Content Creation: - YouTube content - Social media clips - Explainer videos - Creative projects

    Concept Visualization: - Storyboarding - Idea presentation - Proof of concepts - Creative exploration

    AI Persona¶

    Create studio-quality videos with AI avatars and voiceovers in 130+ languages.

    Overview¶

    AI Persona allows you to create professional videos with realistic AI avatars and natural voiceovers. It's as easy as making a slide deck - perfect for presentations, training videos, marketing content, and more.

    Features¶

    AI Avatar Selection: - Multiple professional avatars available - Diverse avatar options - Different poses and settings - Realistic movements and expressions

    Voice Generation: - 130+ languages supported - Natural-sounding voices - Multiple voice options per language - Customizable speech parameters

    Easy Video Creation: - Simple step-by-step process - No video editing required - Professional results instantly - Quick turnaround time

    How to Use AI Persona¶

    Step 1: Select Character (Avatar)

    1. Navigate to Dashboard → AI VIDEO → AI Persona
    2. Browse available avatars

    Avatar Selection: From the screenshot, example avatars include: - Abigail (Upper Body) - Abigail Office Front - Various professional settings - Different poses and backgrounds

    Choose Based On: - Video purpose (professional, casual, educational) - Target audience - Industry/context - Avatar appearance and style

    Step 2: Select Voice

    1. Click "Select a Voice" dropdown
    2. Choose from available voices
    3. Preview voice samples
    4. Select language and accent

    Voice Options: - Male and female voices - Different accents and languages - Professional or conversational tones - Age and personality variations

    Preview Voices: - Click speaker icon to hear sample - Test different voices - Match voice to avatar - Choose appropriate tone

    Step 3: Input Text

    1. Enter text in the "Input Text" field
    2. Type or paste your script
    3. AI will convert text to speech
    4. Avatar will lip-sync to speech

    Text Input Tips: 

    Script Example:

Hello! Welcome to our product demonstration.

Today, I'll show you how our platform can help
streamline your workflow and save you time.

Let's get started with the key features...

    Best Practices: - Write in natural, spoken language - Keep sentences clear and concise - Break into short paragraphs - Use punctuation for natural pauses - Avoid complex technical jargon

    Step 4: Generate Video 1. Review your selections: - Avatar chosen - Voice selected - Text entered 2. Click "Generate Video" button 3. Processing time: 1-3 minutes 4. Preview generated video

    Resolution and Quality¶

    Quality Resolution File Size Use Case
    SD 480p Small Fast preview, testing
    HD 720p Medium Social media, web

    Format Options¶

    MP4 (Recommended) - Universal compatibility - Good compression - Small file sizes - Quality retention

    MOV (Apple) - High quality - Larger files - Best for Apple devices

    AVI (Legacy) - Uncompressed option - Very large files - Maximum quality

    WebM (Web) - Optimized for web - Modern format - Excellent compression

    Platform-Specific Export¶

    YouTube 📺 - Preset: 1080p, 16:9 - Optimal bitrate - Recommended codec - Thumbnail extraction
