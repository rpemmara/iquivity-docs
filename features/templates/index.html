    Workbooks¶

    Workbooks are collections of related templates, documents, and resources organized for specific projects or workflows.

    What are Workbooks?¶

    Think of workbooks as project folders with superpowers:

    • Organized Structure - Multiple sections and pages
    • Mixed Content - Templates, documents, images, notes
    • Collaboration - Work with team members
    • Version Control - Track changes over time
    • Export Options - Share as PDF or link
