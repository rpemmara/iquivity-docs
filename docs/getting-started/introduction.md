# Getting Started with iQuivity

This guide will help you get started with iQuivity quickly and efficiently. Follow these steps to set up your account and begin using the platform.

## Quick Start Guide

### 1. Login to Your Account

Access the iQuivity platform using your credentials.

**What you'll need:**
- Your registered email address
- Your account password
- (Optional) Two-factor authentication code if enabled

[Learn more about Login →](account-creation.md#step-1-login)

---

### 2. Select Your Subscription Plan

Choose a plan that fits your needs based on usage requirements.

**Plan options:**
- **Monthly Plans** - Recurring credits, team features, priority support
- **Prepaid Plans** - One-time token purchase, pay-as-you-go

**What to consider:**
- Number of team members
- Monthly credit needs (Words, Images, Videos, Audio)
- Access to AI Bots and IQ Bots
- Team collaboration requirements

[Learn more about Plans →](account-creation.md#step-2-subscription-if-not-subscribed)

---

### 3. Access Your Dashboard

Once logged in with an active subscription, you'll see your dashboard.

**Dashboard features:**
- View credit usage and resource statistics
- Access all platform tools
- Create documents and content
- Manage IQ Bots and AI Bots
- Collaborate with team members

[Learn more about Dashboard →](dashboard.md)

---

### 4. Configure Your Account

Set up your profile and security settings.

**Recommended setup:**
- Update profile information (name, contact details)
- Enable Two-Factor Authentication for security
- Configure notification preferences
- Connect bank account for payouts (if applicable)

[Learn more about Account Settings →](account-creation.md#step-4-account-settings)

---

### 5. Create Your First IQ Bot

Start increasing your conversion rates immediately.

**Why start with IQ Bot?**
- IQ Bot is the flagship revenue tool
- Consolidates customer touchpoints into one interface
- Eliminates funnel drop-off
- Increases conversion rates by 2-5x

**What you'll create:**
- Unified customer interface with tabs
- AI chatbot for instant engagement
- Calendar, video, document, and form integration
- URL and QR code for easy sharing

[Learn more about Creating IQ Bots →](../chatbots/iq-bot/getting-started.md)

---

## Platform Overview

### Credit-Based System

iQuivity uses a credit-based system for resources:

| Credit Type | Usage |
|-------------|-------|
| **Words** | AI Writer text generation |
| **Images** | AI image creation |
| **Videos** | Video generation and editing |
| **Audio** | Audio content creation |
| **AI Bots** | Website chatbot instances |
| **IQ Bots** | Unified customer interface instances |

Your subscription plan determines your monthly credit allocation. Monitor usage in the "Your Plan" section of your dashboard.

## Tips for Success

### Best Practices

**Start Small:**
1. Create your first IQ Bot to consolidate customer touchpoints
2. Train an AI Bot for customer support
3. Generate initial marketing content
4. Scale up as you become familiar with tools

**Monitor Usage:**
- Check credit usage regularly in dashboard
- Set up low credit alerts
- Upgrade plan when needed

**Leverage Team Features:**
- Invite team members for collaboration
- Assign role-based permissions
- Share workspaces and templates

**Optimize for Revenue:**
- Focus on IQ Bot first for maximum conversion impact
- Use AI Bot to qualify leads and reduce support costs
- Create engaging content to drive traffic to IQ Bots
