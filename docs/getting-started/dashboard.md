## Quick Actions

The Quick Actions panel provides instant access to your most common tasks.

### Available Actions

**New Document**
- Start a new AI-generated text document
- Opens the AI Writer with a blank canvas
- Choose from templates or custom prompts

**Create Image**
- Launch the AI Image Generator
- Start with a text prompt or template
- Generate visual content instantly

**Build Chatbot**
- Create a new chatbot from scratch
- Configure and train your AI assistant
- Deploy to your website

**Upload File**
- Add files to your workspace
- Supported formats: documents, images, videos, and more
- Drag and drop or browse to upload

!!! tip "Keyboard Shortcuts"
    Speed up your workflow with keyboard shortcuts:

    - **âŒ˜/Ctrl + N** - New Document
    - **âŒ˜/Ctrl + U** - Upload File
    - **âŒ˜/Ctrl + K** - Search everything

## Recent Activity

Stay on top of your work with the Recent Activity section, which displays:

### Activity Types

**Recent Content**
- Last 10 documents you created or edited
- Timestamp of last modification
- Quick preview and access

**Ongoing Projects**
- In-progress documents and videos
- Draft chatbots
- Incomplete workbooks

**Collaboration Updates**
- Team member edits and comments
- Shared documents that have been modified
- @mentions and notifications

**File Changes**
- Recently uploaded files
- Modified or shared files
- Deleted items (recoverable from trash)

!!! note "Activity Timeline"
    Your activity feed shows items from the last 30 days. Older items can be found in their respective sections (Files, AI Writer, etc.)

### Storage Used

**Storage Breakdown**
- **Total Storage**: See your storage limit based on your plan
- **Used Space**: Current storage consumption
- **Available**: Remaining storage capacity

**Storage by Type**
- Documents: 30%
- Images: 45%
- Videos: 20%
- Other: 5%

!!! tip "Free Up Space"
    Empty your trash to free up storage. Deleted items are kept for 30 days before permanent deletion.
