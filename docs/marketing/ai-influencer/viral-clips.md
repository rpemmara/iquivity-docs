# AI Influencer - AI Viral Clips

Automatically extract engaging short clips from long-form video content for social media.

---

## Overview

AI Viral Clips uses artificial intelligence to analyze long videos and automatically identify the most engaging moments. Transform podcasts, webinars, interviews, and other long-form content into bite-sized viral clips perfect for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and other short-form platforms.

!!! info "AI-Powered Clip Detection"
    Advanced AI analyzes your video content to identify viral-worthy moments, emotional peaks, key quotes, and engaging segments automatically.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Video Upload Failed:**
- **Cause:** File too large, unsupported format, connection issue
- **Solution:**
  - Compress video to under 2GB
  - Convert to MP4 format
  - Check internet connection
  - Use URL upload instead
  - Try splitting into smaller segments

**Poor Clip Quality:**
- **Cause:** Low-resolution source, compression artifacts
- **Solution:**
  - Use higher quality source video
  - Upload original files (not compressed)
  - Select higher quality export settings
  - Avoid multiple re-encodes

**No Clips Generated:**
- **Cause:** Video too short, poor audio, no speech detected
- **Solution:**
  - Ensure video is at least 5 minutes
  - Check audio track exists and is audible
  - Verify language selection is correct
  - Test with different video
  - Contact support with video details

**Subtitles Inaccurate:**
- **Cause:** Poor audio quality, accents, background noise, wrong language
- **Solution:**
  - Verify correct language selected
  - Use video with clearer audio
  - Manually edit subtitles after generation
  - Remove background music/noise from source
  - Consider professional audio cleanup

**Processing Taking Too Long:**
- **Cause:** Long video, high clip count, server load
- **Solution:**
  - Reduce number of clips requested
  - Process during off-peak hours
  - Split video into segments
  - Upgrade to priority processing
  - Check account tier limits
