# AI Chat Pro - Overview

AI Chat Pro is your personal AI assistant interface within iQuivity, providing access to multiple cutting-edge AI models for productivity, creativity, and problem-solving.

---

## What is AI Chat Pro?

**AI Chat Pro** is iQuivity's internal chatbot interface designed for your own work and productivity. Unlike IQ Bot and AI Bot (which you deploy for customers), AI Chat Pro is your personal workspace to interact with various AI models.

!!! info "Personal AI Workspace"
    Think of AI Chat Pro as your own ChatGPT or Claude interface, but with access to multiple AI models and integrated with your iQuivity account.

---

## Key Differences: AI Chat Pro vs Other Chatbots

| Feature | AI Chat Pro | IQ Bot | AI Bot |
|---------|-------------|---------|---------|
| **Purpose** | Personal productivity | Standalone mini-site for customers | Website widget for customers |
| **Access** | You use it | Customers access via URL/QR | Customers access on your website |
| **AI Models** | Multiple models available | Uses your selected model | Uses your selected model |
| **Training** | Pre-configured experts | Custom training data | Custom training data |
| **Deployment** | Internal use only | Public URL/QR code | Embedded on website |

## Getting Started

### Accessing AI Chat Pro

**Navigate to AI Chat Pro:**

1. From the main dashboard, locate **"AI Chat Pro"** in the left-hand navigation
2. Click to open the chat interface
3. Select your preferred AI model or expert chatbot
4. Start chatting!

### Quick Start Guide

**Step 1: Choose Your Chatbot**
- Click the chatbot dropdown to see all available bots
- Select "Default AI Chat Bot" for general use
- Or choose a specialized expert (Finance, Nutritionist, etc.)

**Step 2: Select AI Model**
- Click on the AI Model selector
- Browse available models
- Select based on your needs (speed vs capability)
- Click "Apply"

**Step 3: Start Chatting**
- Type your question or request
- Use prompt suggestions for ideas
- Get instant AI-powered responses

## Privacy & Security

### Conversation Privacy

**Temporary Chat Mode:**
- Enable for sensitive conversations
- Won't be saved to chat history
- Content may still appear in content manager
- Useful for confidential information

**Regular Chats:**
- Saved to your conversation history
- Accessible only to you (unless shared)
- Can be exported or deleted

### Data Handling

**Your Data:**
- Conversations stored in your iQuivity account
- Not used for AI model training (check AI provider terms)
- Can be exported or deleted anytime
- Team sharing is controlled by you

## Frequently Asked Questions

??? question "Can I use AI Chat Pro for commercial purposes?"
    Yes! AI Chat Pro is designed for your business and personal productivity. Use it to create content, analyze data, generate code, and more.

??? question "How is this different from using ChatGPT directly?"
    AI Chat Pro gives you access to multiple AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Perplexity, DeepSeek, Grok, OpenRouter) in one interface, integrated with your iQuivity account, with pre-configured experts and business features like brand voice and team sharing.

??? question "Do conversations use my iQuivity credits?"
    Most models offer unlimited credits. Some premium features may use credits. Check the model card for specific credit requirements.

??? question "Can I share conversations with my team?"
    Yes! Use the Share feature to share conversations with team members within your iQuivity account.

??? question "What's the difference between regular chat and temporary chat?"
    Temporary chats won't be saved to your conversation history, useful for sensitive or one-time queries. Regular chats are saved and can be accessed later.

??? question "Can I upload files for analysis?"
    Yes! AI Chat Pro supports file uploads including PDFs, images, text files, code, and data files for analysis and discussion.
