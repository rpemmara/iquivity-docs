## Billing FAQ

**Q: When will I be charged?**
A: On the same date each month/year based on your initial subscription date.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: We offer refunds within 14 days of initial purchase only. Contact support@iquivity.ai.

**Q: What happens if my payment fails?**
A: We'll retry 3 times over 7 days. If all fail, your account is suspended until payment is updated.

**Q: Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?**
A: Yes! Annual billing offers 20% discount. Switch anytime in billing settings.

**Q: Do unused credits roll over?**
A: No, monthly credits reset each billing cycle. Purchase credit add-ons if you need credits that don't expire.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: Not currently, but you can downgrade to the Free plan and resubscribe when ready.
