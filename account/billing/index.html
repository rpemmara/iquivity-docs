    Subscription Plans & Billing¶

    Manage your subscription, billing, and payment methods. Understand credit usage and optimize your plan.

    Understanding Plans¶

    iQuivity AI offers multiple subscription tiers to meet different needs.

    Free Plan¶

    Perfect for trying out iQuivity AI

    ✅ Included: - 100 monthly credits - 1 GB storage - AI Writer (basic features) - AI Image Generator (standard quality) - 1 chatbot - Community support

    ❌ Limitations: - No team collaboration - No API access - No video generation - Limited templates - Ads on chatbot

    Starter Plan - $29/month¶

    Great for individuals and freelancers

    ✅ Everything in Free, plus: - 1,000 monthly credits - 10 GB storage - Full AI Writer access - High-quality image generation - 3 chatbots (no ads) - AI Video Creator (basic) - Basic API access (100 requests/hour) - Email support - All templates

    Professional Plan - $99/month¶

    Ideal for professionals and small teams

    ✅ Everything in Starter, plus: - 10,000 monthly credits - 100 GB storage - Team collaboration (up to 5 members) - 10 chatbots - Full video features - Full API access (1,000 requests/hour) - Priority processing - Priority support (24-hour response) - Advanced analytics - Custom templates - Remove branding

    Most Popular - Best value for growing teams!

    Enterprise Plan - Custom Pricing¶

    For large organizations with advanced needs

    ✅ Everything in Professional, plus: - Custom credit allocation - Unlimited storage (or custom) - Unlimited team members - Unlimited chatbots - Custom API rate limits - Dedicated account manager - Custom integrations - SSO (Single Sign-On) - Advanced security features - SLA guarantees - Custom contracts - White-label options - Custom AI model training

    Contact sales: sales@iquivity.ai

    Upgrading Your Plan¶

    Benefits of Upgrading: - More credits for AI operations - Increased storage capacity - Additional features unlocked - Better support response time - Team collaboration features

    How to Upgrade:

    1. Go to Settings → Billing → Plans
    2. Click "Upgrade" on desired plan
    3. Review plan comparison
    4. Click "Upgrade to [Plan Name]"
    5. Confirm upgrade

    Downgrading Your Plan¶

    Before Downgrading:

    ⚠️ Consider These Impacts: - Reduced credit allocation - Less storage space (may need to delete files) - Fewer team member seats - Limited chatbots - Reduced API access - Lost premium features

    How to Downgrade:

    1. Go to Settings → Billing
    2. Click "Change Plan"
    3. Select lower-tier plan

    Credits System¶

    Understanding Credits¶

    What Are Credits? - Credits are the currency for AI operations - Each AI operation consumes credits - Credits reset monthly based on your plan - Unused credits don't roll over

    Credit Costs:

    Operation Credit Cost
    Text Generation
    Short (< 300 words) 1-2
    Medium (300-800 words) 3-5
    Long (800-2000 words) 6-10
    Image Generation
    Standard (512×512) 10
    High (1024×1024) 25
    Ultra (2048×2048) 50
    Video Creation
    30-60 seconds 50-75
    1-3 minutes 100-150
    AI Viral Clip 30 per clip
    Chatbot
    Per conversation message 1-5
    API Calls
    Per request Varies by endpoint

    Billing FAQ¶

    Q: When will I be charged? A: On the same date each month/year based on your initial subscription date.

    Q: Can I get a refund? A: We offer refunds within 14 days of initial purchase only. Contact support@iquivity.ai.

    Q: What happens if my payment fails? A: We'll retry 3 times over 7 days. If all fail, your account is suspended until payment is updated.

    Q: Can I switch between monthly and annual billing? A: Yes! Annual billing offers 20% discount. Switch anytime in billing settings.

    Q: Do unused credits roll over? A: No, monthly credits reset each billing cycle. Purchase credit add-ons if you need credits that don't expire.

    Q: Can I pause my subscription? A: Not currently, but you can downgrade to the Free plan and resubscribe when ready.
