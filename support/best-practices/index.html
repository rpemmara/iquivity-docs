    Tips & Best Practices¶

    Maximize your productivity and get the best results from iQuivity AI with these proven tips and best practices.

    AI Content Generation¶

    Writing Effective Prompts¶

    The quality of AI output depends heavily on your prompts.

    Prompt Formula¶

    Context + Task + Format + Constraints + Examples = Perfect Prompt

    Example:

    ‚ùå Bad Prompt: 

    "Write about marketing"

    ‚úÖ Good Prompt: 

    "As a B2B SaaS marketing expert, write a 500-word blog post about
email marketing automation for small business owners. Use a friendly
but professional tone. Include 3 specific benefits with real-world
examples. Target audience: non-technical business owners with limited
marketing experience."

    Prompt Best Practices¶

    1. Be Specific - State exactly what you want - Include details about length, tone, audience - Specify format and structure

    2. Provide Context - Who is the audience? - What is the purpose? - Where will this be used?

    3. Set Constraints - Word count or length limits - Tone and style requirements - What to include/exclude - Format requirements

    4. Use Examples - Show the style you want - Reference similar content - Describe the desired outcome

    5. Iterate - Start with base prompt - Refine based on results - Save successful prompts

    Optimizing Content Quality¶

    Review and Edit - Never use AI content without review - Always fact-check information - Add your personal touch - Verify accuracy

    Use the Right Tone 

    Professional: Business documents, proposals, reports
Casual: Social media, blog posts, emails
Friendly: Customer communications, welcome messages
Formal: Legal documents, academic writing
Creative: Marketing copy, storytelling

    Specify Your Audience - General public - Industry professionals - Technical experts - Beginners - Executives - Students

    Leverage Templates - Start with proven templates - Customize for your needs - Create custom templates for reuse - Share best templates with team

    Credit Optimization¶

    Save Credits Strategically¶

    1. Use Appropriate Settings - Don't use "long" if "medium" suffices - Start with standard resolution for images - Use lower creativity for straightforward content

    2. Batch Similar Tasks - Generate multiple variations at once - More efficient than individual requests - Use bulk operations when available

    3. Refine Prompts First - Test with short outputs first - Perfect your prompt before full generation - Use test mode if available

    4. Use Improve Features Wisely - Edit specific sections instead of regenerating all - Use "Improve" on paragraphs vs entire document - Selective enhancements save credits

    5. Leverage Templates - Templates are optimized for efficiency - Pre-tested prompts use fewer credits - Consistent results with less trial and error

    Credit Usage Guidelines¶

    Cost-Effective Operations: 

    ‚úÖ Use templates (optimized prompts)
‚úÖ Edit and improve sections (vs full regeneration)
‚úÖ Standard image resolution for drafts
‚úÖ Shorter content for testing

    Account Security¶

    Enable 2FA - Use authenticator app (not SMS) - Save backup codes securely - Regular security reviews

    Password Management - Use unique, strong passwords - Minimum 12 characters - Password manager recommended - Change every 6 months

    Session Management - Review active sessions monthly - End unknown sessions immediately - Don't stay logged in on shared devices
