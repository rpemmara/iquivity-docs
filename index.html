    Your Complete AI Platform for Revenue Growth¶

    iQuivity is a comprehensive cloud-based AI platform designed to increase your conversion rates and drive revenue growth by eliminating funnel drop-off and enhancing customer engagement.

    The Revenue Challenge¶

    The Conversion Problem

    Average click-through conversion rates are only 2-3% - meaning 97-98% of potential customers drop off without converting.

    Why? Digital fragmentation forces users to navigate between multiple separate systems, creating friction at every step and massive abandonment opportunities.

    The iQuivity Solution: IQ Bot¶

    Eliminate Funnel Drop-Off, Increase Revenue¶

    IQ Bot is our flagship revenue-generation tool that solves the funnel drop-off problem by consolidating all customer touchpoints into a single, unified interface.

      AI-powered mini-site that eliminates digital fragmentation by presenting websites, calendars, videos, documents, chat, and forms in one seamless URL-accessible interface.
